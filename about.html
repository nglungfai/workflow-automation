About this site

Maintained by the Product team

This site is a working knowledge base for using Claude Cowork at Predactiv — practical tutorials, workflow write-ups, and prompting tips aimed at helping the team get real value out of AI tooling during our transformation. It's also the home of the Predactiv AI Reading List.

Who this is for

Everyone at Predactiv. The examples lean toward Product, Data, and Engineering because that's where we started — but the core workflows (meeting notes into action items, status updates, document drafting, research synthesis) apply to any role, and Getting Started assumes no technical background. If you're in sales, marketing, ops, or finance, the "Start here" steps below work exactly the same for you.

Start here

  1. Read Getting Started with Claude Cowork — what it is, how it differs from a normal chat, and what it can safely touch.
  2. Run its five-minute prompt against one of your own Drive folders — it's read-only, nothing gets modified, and it's the fastest way to build intuition.
  3. Skim the reading list's "Applying AI in our work" lane — the ideas behind the workflows, each with a note on how it applies here.

The Reading List

Alongside the tutorials, this site hosts the Predactiv AI Reading List — the canonical, monthly-curated set of AI reading for the team, in three lanes: research papers, engineering write-ups, and applying AI in our work. Each entry notes how it applies at Predactiv, and entries cross-link with the tutorials here: the list is the theory, the posts are how we put it to work. To suggest an entry, ping the Product team or open a pull request.

How this site is made

Cowork isn't just the subject of this site — it's the co-author. Posts are drafted, formatted, cross-linked, and published through the same workflows the tutorials teach: a person describes the deliverable, Cowork does the drafting and the mechanics, and nothing ships without human review. The reading list runs on the same loop — a scheduled monthly review proposes updates, and a person approves them before anything changes.
