Research papers

The science — foundational methods and applied research behind audience modeling, response, and measurement.

01

Audience Expansion for Online Social Network Advertising

Liu et al. (LinkedIn) · 2016Core

LinkedIn documents its production system for growing a campaign's target audience to similar members, comparing a campaign-agnostic and a campaign-aware method plus a hybrid, validated with large-scale online experiments.

Apply at Predactiv: A direct reference architecture for our lookalike product — the agnostic-vs-aware split maps straight onto our reach/precision tradeoff.

02

Efficient Estimation of Word Representations (Word2Vec)

Mikolov et al. · 2013Core

Introduces skip-gram and CBOW, two efficient models that learn word vectors from large corpora, producing embeddings whose geometry captures meaningful semantic and syntactic relationships.

Apply at Predactiv: Train user2vec / prod2vec on event logs, then nearest-neighbor from the seed set to expand audiences.

03

Practical Lessons from Predicting Clicks on Ads at Facebook

He et al. (Facebook) · 2014Core

Facebook shows that feeding gradient-boosted-tree outputs into a linear classifier sharply improves ad-click prediction, and shares lessons on data freshness and sampling.

Apply at Predactiv: A cheap, strong baseline for conversion/CTR scoring — reach for this before deep nets.

04

A Literature Survey of the State-of-the-Art in Uplift Modeling

Devriendt, Moldovan, Verbeke · 2018Core

A structured review of uplift methods, which estimate the incremental effect of a treatment per individual, with an experimental comparison across approaches and datasets.

Apply at Predactiv: The discipline that separates real audience value from correlation — essential for defensible incrementality claims to clients.

Engineering write-ups

How industry teams actually built it — practitioner posts, more readable than papers, each linking to the original.

05

Real-time Personalization using Embeddings for Search Ranking

Airbnb · Grbovic et al. · 2018Core

Airbnb explains how it learns embeddings for listings and users from booking search sessions, then uses nearest-neighbor lookups to personalize search results in real time.

Apply at Predactiv: Close to our lookalike problem — the session-embedding recipe transfers directly to building audience vectors from event streams.

06

PinnerSage: Multi-Modal User Embedding

Pinterest · 2020Core

Pinterest represents each user with multiple embeddings — clusters of their distinct interests — rather than one averaged vector, improving recommendation quality.

Apply at Predactiv: A better representation for multi-interest audiences — avoids the averaging that blurs seed segments.

07

Meet Michelangelo: Uber's Machine Learning Platform

Uber · 2017Intro

Uber introduces Michelangelo, its internal end-to-end ML platform spanning feature storage, training, evaluation, and serving — the post that popularized the pattern.

Apply at Predactiv: A blueprint for how Predactiv's ML and DE teams industrialize model delivery and feature reuse.

08

150 Successful Machine Learning Models: 6 Lessons Learned

Booking.com · Bernardi et al. · 2019Core

Booking.com distills lessons from deploying 150 models in production, including the finding that offline metric gains frequently fail to translate into business value.

Apply at Predactiv: A sober checklist for our model program — judge audience models on business lift, not offline AUC.

09

It's All A/Bout Testing: The Netflix Experimentation Platform

Netflix · 2016Intro

Netflix's accessible overview of how it treats experimentation as core infrastructure and culture, running controlled tests throughout the product.

Apply at Predactiv: The organizational model for making incrementality testing routine at Predactiv.

10

Under the Hood of Uber's Experimentation Platform

Uber · 2018Core

Uber details the architecture that lets it run thousands of concurrent experiments reliably, covering assignment, statistics engines, and monitoring.

Apply at Predactiv: A reference architecture if Predactiv builds its own measurement and experimentation tooling.

11

Private Lift: Privacy-Enhancing Measurement

Meta · 2021Core

Meta describes privacy-preserving measurement using secure multiparty computation, letting advertisers measure campaign lift without either side exposing raw user data.

Apply at Predactiv: The trust architecture for partner measurement — measure incrementality across datasets without moving identities.

12

Privacy Sandbox: Attribution Reporting API

Google · 2022Intro

Google's framework for measuring ad conversions without cross-site identifiers, relying on aggregated and noised reports instead of user-level tracking.

Apply at Predactiv: Required reading for a cookieless measurement roadmap — the constraints our measurement products must live within.
